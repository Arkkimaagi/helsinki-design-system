<LargeParagraph>Helsinki Design System is an open-source design system for digital products and experiences.</LargeParagraph>

The Helsinki Design System helps teams work and communicate more efficiently. It acts as a single source of truth for designers and developers, providing shared guidelines, design tokens and reusable components to make all websites of Helsinki City more cohesive.

The Helsinki Design System consists of several parts:
- **Component library** that helps developers quickly create the best experience for City of Helsinki’s digital services. 
- **Design kit** that provides design files of HDS components for designers, and helps designers to design beautiful and accessible experiences, that follow the City of Helsinki brand.
- **Visual assets** like icons and logos help delivering a cohesive visual expereince for users.
- **Documentation and guidelines** include detailled information on how to design and implement the HDS components, design tokens and visual assets: Design principles, accessibility guidelines, usage practices and examples, interactive demos and API descpritions.


## One Hel of an impact
Helsinki is a place where you can make a difference for the good of the whole world, with people who are passionate about making a difference. In the future, Helsinki will be better known for its influential people, acts and encounters. 

Our attitude *”One Hel of an impact”* challenges you to think big, encourages you to carry out exceptional deeds and create impactful digital services. It gives you the opportunity to make Helsinki to stand out from other cities. 

The cornerstones of the City of Helsinki brand are:
- [The Helsinki framed logo](https://brand.hel.fi/logo "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Logo").
- The prorpietary [Helsinki Grotesk typeface](https://brand.hel.fi/typography "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Typography").
- Bold [brand colours](https://brand.hel.fi/colours "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Colour"), inspired by the Helsinki's urban landscape.
- The [wave motifs](https://brand.hel.fi/wave-motifs "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Wave mortifs"), [icons](https://brand.hel.fi/icons-and-pictograms "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Icons and pictograms"), and other recognizable graphic elements.
- Impactfull use of [photography](https://brand.hel.fi/en/photograph-guidelines/ "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines / Photograph guidelines")

See more details about the brand of City of Helsinki, in the [Visual Identity Guidelines](https://brand.hel.fi/ "City of Helsinki Visual Identity Guidelines").
